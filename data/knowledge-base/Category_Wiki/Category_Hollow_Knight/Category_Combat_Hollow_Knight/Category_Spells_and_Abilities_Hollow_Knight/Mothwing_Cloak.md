# Mothwing Cloak

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Mothwing_Cloak

The Mothwing Cloak is an Ability in Hollow Knight . It allows dashing forward a short distance.

Function

Press DASH to dash a short distance in the direction the Knight is facing. Can be used in mid-air. The cooldown between dashes is 0.6 seconds. Refreshes when the Knight lands on the ground, bounces off an enemy /object with their Nail , or clings to/jumps off a wall using the Mantis Claw .

Allows the Knight to cross gaps too far to reach by jumping, and makes it easier to dodge enemies and their attacks.

Modifiers

Can be upgraded to the Shade Cloak .

The Dashmaster Charm reduces the cooldown between dashes from 0.6 seconds to 0.4 seconds. It also allows the Knight to dash downwards.

How to Acquire

Found on the body of a Vessel after defeating Hornet in Greenpath in the boss arena.

Trivia

The original name for this item on the Kickstarter page was the Mothdust Cloak.
