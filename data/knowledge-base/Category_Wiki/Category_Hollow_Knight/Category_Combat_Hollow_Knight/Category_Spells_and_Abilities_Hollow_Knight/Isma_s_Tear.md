# Isma's Tear

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Isma's_Tear

Isma's Tear is an Ability in Hollow Knight . It allows the Knight to swim in acid without taking damage.

Function

Although very useful, Isma's Tear is not necessary to complete the game. Obtaining it allows access to the following:

Shape of Unn in Greenpath Access to Fog Canyon through Forgotten Crossroads or Fungal Wastes A Grub in Isma's Grove Access to the Queen's Gardens A Grub as well as a shortcut in Kingdom's Edge

It is also useful while exploring Fog Canyon and fighting Uumuu .

How to Acquire

Located in Isma's Grove in the Royal Waterways . Accessing this area requires Crystal Heart and beating Dung Defender . A separate,much harder way to access it is by dashing, pogo-bouncing on the spikes then immediately dashing again. This method is almost impossible without frame-perfect timing.

Trivia

Dung Defender refers to it as "Isma's Blessing".
