# Desolate Dive

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Desolate_Dive

Desolate Dive is a Spell in Hollow Knight . Using it strikes the ground with concentrated SOUL , breaking floors and damaging enemies .

Function

Press FOCUS/CAST or QUICK CAST while holding DOWN to cause the Knight to slam into the ground, creating a shockwave that spreads out and up. The dive deals 15 damage, while the shockwave deals 20.

The Knight is invincible for 0.4 seconds during and after using Desolate Dive. This allows for chaining uses of Desolate Dive to avoid enemy damage as long as the Knight has enough SOUL. Using Desolate Dive in quick succession cancels the invincibility frames of the Spell if it is cast while the previous Spell's invincibility frames have not yet run out.

Desolate Dive also allows breaking certain floors, such as those to exit the Soul Sanctum and to enter Crystal Peak .

Modifiers

Shaman Stone increases the damage dealt by Desolate Dive by 51%. Spell Twister lowers the SOUL cost to cast Desolate Dive to 24 SOUL. Can be upgraded to Descending Dark .

How to Acquire

Reward for defeating Soul Master in the City of Tears .

Trivia

Desolate Dive was revealed in a Kickstarter update where it was referred to as the Tyrant's Fist :

"This ancient, royal magic allows you to focus energy into your fist and slam it down into the ground, releasing a shockwave that blows away foes and can even rupture the earth you stand on!"

Descending Dark and Desolate Dive can be used while Dung Defender is underground to force him out and stagger him. After obtaining Desolate Dive, the Knight's SOUL is completely refilled.
