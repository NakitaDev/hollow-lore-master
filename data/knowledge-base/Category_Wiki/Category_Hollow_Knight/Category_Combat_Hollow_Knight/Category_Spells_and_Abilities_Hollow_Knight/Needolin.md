# Needolin

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Needolin

Needolin is a Weaver Talent Hornet unlocks in Act 1 of Silksong . Using it plays music while slowly draining the Silk Spool.

Function

Hold down the Needolin button to start playing music. Music will continue until you let go or all of your Silk is expended.

Using it near a Weavenest Door allows you to enter. These are important places that give you tremendous benefits.

Near NPCs, they will start singing along and reveal hidden dialogue, much like the Dream Nail from Hollow Knight .

In combat scenarios, the Needolin will pacify enemies and also reveal hidden dialogue from them.

Needolin can only be used on the ground. Getting attacked cancels the ability.

Modifiers

In Act 3 , you will unlock 2 new abilities for the Needolin. The first modifier you will unlock is the Beastling Call . This allows you to instantly travel back to the Bell Beast from almost anywhere. Unlocked after defeating the Bell Eater . The second modifier you will unlock is the Elegy of the Deep . Required for obtaining the 3 hearts to continue with the story. This also allows you to explore Lost Verdania. After defeating the Green Prince , it is no longer accessible Using the Spider Strings tool increases the aforementioned functions of the Needolin

Trivia

Hornet can be seen playing the Needolin in the Reveal Trailer, next to the Chapel Maiden .
