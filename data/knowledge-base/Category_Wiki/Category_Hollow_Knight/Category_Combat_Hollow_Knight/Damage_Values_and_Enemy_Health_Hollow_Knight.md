Health Values

The following is a list of all enemies in the game and their health values.

Enemies with 1 health may not necessarily have 1 HP, but are immune to tick damage (such as Spore Shroom) and have low enough health that the starting Nail kills them in 1 hit. Enemies with "—" health cannot be damaged. A forward slash / represents a division of health from that enemy's multiple health pools. Parentheses () clarify the nature of that enemy's health. Certain enemies and bosses gain HP after the Knight upgrades the Old Nail. Enemies with this change are noted in parentheses.
