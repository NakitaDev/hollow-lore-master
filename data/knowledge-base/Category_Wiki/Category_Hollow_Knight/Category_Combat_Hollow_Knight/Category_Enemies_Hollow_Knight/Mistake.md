# Mistake

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Mistake

Mistakes are enemies in Hollow Knight . Former scholars of the Soul Sanctum , these bugs' bodies were deformed by the Sanctum's experiments with Soul .

Behaviour and Tactics

Meanders back and forth, charging at the Knight when in range. May occasionally appear out of the ground.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Large numbers of them are located in the Soul Sanctum, occasionally appearing out of the floor. Three Mistakes also spawn in the Colosseum of Fools during the 9th wave of the Trial of the Fool .

Gallery

Trivia

The Mistake is one of the few enemies that respawns upon leaving and re-entering the room. The Mistake is one of the few enemies that has various different looks, but no change to behaviour.
