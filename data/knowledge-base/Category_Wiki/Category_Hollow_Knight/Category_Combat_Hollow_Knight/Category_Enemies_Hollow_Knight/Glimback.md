# Glimback

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Glimback

Glimbacks are enemies in Hollow Knight . They used to be beasts of burden who were forced to carry around carts and machines.

Behaviour and Tactics

Slowly meanders back and forth, ignoring the Knight .

Its hardened shell grants protection from all sides but its face and its belly. It is often located in tight corridors, so getting caught between it and a wall should be avoided.

Spells pass through its armour and damage its face.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

The Glimback, although not very common, can be found mainly in Crystal Peak ; however, two of them can be spotted in the lower Crystal Peak - Forgotten Crossroads entrance.

Trivia

The Glimback is one of the few enemies that respawns after leaving and re-entering the room. One of the screenshots featured in the official Hollow Knight Press-Kit depicts a Glimback walking up the right wall of Crystal Guardian's challenge room. However, none of the Glimbacks found in the final release of the game have this ability.
