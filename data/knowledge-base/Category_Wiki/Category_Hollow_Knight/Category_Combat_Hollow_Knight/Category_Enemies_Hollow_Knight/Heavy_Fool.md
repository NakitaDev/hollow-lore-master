# Heavy Fool

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Heavy_Fool

Heavy Fools are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are bulky warriors who fight for their lives in the Colosseum of Fools .

Behaviour and Tactics

Heavy Fools have three attacks:

Forward Slash : They unleash a powerful swing in an arc in front of them. Charge : They charge at the Knight with their nail held in front of them. Leap : When the Knight attempts to jump over them, they react by hopping in the air, dealing contact damage. As they land on the ground, they perform a Forward Slash attack.

It is recommended to burst them down quickly, as they are among the most dangerous Fools with their wide variety of attacks available.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Heavy Fools are only found in the Colosseum of Fools, in the second and third trial.
