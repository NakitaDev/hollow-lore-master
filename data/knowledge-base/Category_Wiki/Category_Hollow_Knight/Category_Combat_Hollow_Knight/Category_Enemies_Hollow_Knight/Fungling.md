# Fungling

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Fungling

Funglings are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are gas-filled balloon creatures who ignore the world around them. Funglings are juveniles, becoming Fungoons upon reaching adulthood.

Behaviour and Tactics

Floats and often drifts about peacefully, but deals contact damage to the Knight .

Easily avoided due to their lack of aggression, Funglings are mainly used for platforming by Nail-bouncing on them while in the air. They also serve as obstacles during platforming challenge segments and must be avoided or dealt with.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

They are found in large groups throughout most of the Fungal Wastes and the Fungal Core . Some can also be found in the northeast part of the Junk Pit .
