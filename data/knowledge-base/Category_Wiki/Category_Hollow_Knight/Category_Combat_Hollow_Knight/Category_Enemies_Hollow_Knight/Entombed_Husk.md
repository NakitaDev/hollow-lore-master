# Entombed Husk

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Entombed_Husk

Entombed Husks are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are decaying mummified corpses which became enraged by the Infection . Hiding in the darkness, they wait for creatures to come close to them.

Behaviour and Tactics

Slowly drags itself about and charges quickly towards the Knight once in range, swiping three times while pulling itself forward. It deals two Masks of damage on contact.

Keep an eye on the distance between the Knight and the enemy, and quickly back up to avoid any head-on confrontation. Also, make sure to use any height advantages, if possible. Its eyes glow and it makes choking noises as it drags itself around, so it can easily be heard from far away.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Found in the crypts beneath the Resting Grounds .
