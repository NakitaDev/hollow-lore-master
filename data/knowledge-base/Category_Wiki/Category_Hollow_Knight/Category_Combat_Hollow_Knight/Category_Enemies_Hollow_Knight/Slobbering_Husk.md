# Slobbering Husk

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Slobbering_Husk

Slobbering Husks are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are husks that were completely consumed by Infection , overfilling with noxious corrosive liquid.

Behaviour and Tactics

Despite physically resembling the Leaping Husk , this enemy does not jump, instead, it spits a cluster of corrosive blobs in an arc that linger temporarily on the ground.

Its attack is predictable and easy to dodge. Avoid one spit and move in quickly for a kill, or attack with a ranged Spell or a Nail Art with a large area of effect, such as Dash Slash .

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Since the Leaping Husks in Forgotten Crossroads mostly become Slobbering Husks after the Infection spreads, their locations are similar.

Trivia

Unlike other Infected Crossroads enemies, Slobbering Husks do not explode upon death. Its body still glows orange upon death like all other exploding corpses, possibly suggesting that its ability to explode was originally intended but then later removed at some point.
