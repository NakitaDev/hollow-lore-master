# Mosscreep

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Mosscreep

Mosscreeps are enemies in Hollow Knight . They camouflage themselves with leaves and hide from danger amongst the undergrowth.

Behaviour and Tactics

Hides in greenery until the Knight approaches. Once approached they shuffle about in their bush before rising up and wandering back and forth.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

They are found in abundance in Greenpath and the Queen's Gardens .

Gallery

Trivia

The Mosscreep is one of the few enemies that respawns upon leaving and re-entering the room. There is a glitch where, if the Knight opens the Map while a Mosscreep is moving upside-down towards the edge of a platform, it continues moving, off the platform and through the air.
