# Hive Guardian

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Hive_Guardian

Hive Guardians are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are huge bees whose only instinct is to guard the Hive by charging at intruders.

Behaviour and Tactics

Simply charges at the Knight , dealing 2 Masks of damage and rebounding off any terrain in its way. They can destroy cracked platforms, which can make platforming sections more difficult. Their massive size combined with their erratic bouncing movements makes them tricky to predict and avoid. Focus on them and destroy them as quickly as possible.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

They are found in small groups within the Hive, usually near Hive Soldiers .

Trivia

With the Hiveblood Charm equipped, they do not attack the Knight nor deal damage on contact, however, they still retaliate when attacked. In order to get the Mask Shard in the Hive, it is necessary to bait a Hive Guardian into breaking a cracked wall, as the Hunter hints in his Journal entry.
