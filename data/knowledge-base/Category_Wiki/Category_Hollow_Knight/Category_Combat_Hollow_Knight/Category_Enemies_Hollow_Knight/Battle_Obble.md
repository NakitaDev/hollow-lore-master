# Battle Obble

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_Obble

Battle Obbles are enemies in Hollow Knight . These bulbous creatures were raised in the Colosseum of Fools for combat. The Obble is their wild relative.

Behaviour and Tactics

Flies around in random patterns and intermittently fires four blobs of Infection in an X-shaped pattern. Also fires after taking any damage.

It does not directly target the Knight , but multiple spawn at once in the Colosseum, making dodging their shots difficult. The best strategy is to take them down quickly with a Nail Art or Spell (and possibly hit multiple at once). It is always recommended to stay beneath or directly beside this enemy, as its shots cannot hit the Knight.

They have far more health than regular Obbles; even a Great Slash from a Pure Nail fails to kill them in one hit.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Battle Obbles are only found in the Colosseum of Fools, in the second and last Trials.
