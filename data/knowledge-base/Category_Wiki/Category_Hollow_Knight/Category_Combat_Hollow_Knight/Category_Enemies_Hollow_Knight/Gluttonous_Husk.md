# Gluttonous Husk

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Gluttonous_Husk

Gluttonous Husks are enemies in Hollow Knight . In life, these upper-class bugs consumed so much food that they took on a grotesque shape. Reanimated after death, they now use their body to attack.

Behaviour and Tactics

Meanders back and forth until disturbed. When engaged, it hops a short distance in the Knight's direction, dealing contact damage.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

They are mainly found in the east part of the City of Tears , much like the Cowardly Husk and Husk Dandy .
