# Mantis Petra

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Mantis_Petra

Mantis Petras are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are flying Infected Mantises of the Mantis Tribe who followed the Traitor Lord and were exiled from their village .

Behaviour and Tactics

Hovers just outside the Knight's melee reach. It flings scythes that swoop down in a parabolic arc, slow at the apex, and then come back up.

If possible, try to get slightly above them to lure them close and hit them with a Nail Art or Spell before they hover away again. Alternatively, use Howling Wraiths / Abyss Shriek or the Grubberfly's Elegy Charm to close the distance.

They often show up in groups with another Petra or a Mantis Traitor . Get rid of them first, as their aerial attacks make fighting ground-based enemies more difficult.

Dreamshield destroys their scythes when making contact, making the Charm useful against them.

Location

The Mantis Petra are very commonly found in the Queen's Gardens , usually as a challenge in the platforming sections. Also found within the Colosseum of Fools , in the 9th, 14th and 17th waves of the Trial of the Fool .

Trivia

Mantis Petras used to have the same Dream Nail dialogue as hostile Mantis Youths . This was fixed in version 1.5.68.11808 . The name "Mantis Petra" is technically incorrect, since in Greek, "petra" (πέτρα) means rock or stone, while "pterix/pteryga" (πτέρυξ/πτέρυγα) means wing, the intention in this case being "winged mantis". This, however, is likely to have been a conscious choice by Team Cherry, since "mantis ptera" is more difficult to pronounce.
