# Sharp Baldur

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Sharp_Baldur

Sharp Baldurs are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are juvenile Baldurs with a plated shell who were bred to fight in the Colosseum of Fools .

Behaviour and Tactics

Behaves like normal Baldurs; curling into a ball and rolling towards the Knight , then continuing to bounce off walls for a short time afterwards. The only difference is that Sharp Baldurs have more health.

The best tactic to fight them is to simply attack them from the front as they roll beneath, as the knockback they receive is enough to keep them at bay. Using the Quick Slash Charm is useful for keeping this up.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

This enemy is only found in the Colosseum of Fools, in the first and third trials, but it can also be spawned by the Collector in the Hall of Gods on Ascended difficulty or above, and in the Pantheon of Hallownest .

Trivia

The Sharp Baldur, unlike the regular Baldur, does not have an Elder counterpart . This is pointed out by the Hunter in his journal, who supposes they may not live long enough to reach adult age.
