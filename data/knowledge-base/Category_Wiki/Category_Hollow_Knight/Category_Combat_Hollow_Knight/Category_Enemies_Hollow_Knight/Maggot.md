# Maggot

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Maggot

Maggots are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are helpless and weak creatures who were forced to do menial labour.

Behaviour and Tactics

Flees from the Knight while blubbering, posing no threat. Runs until it hits a wall then ricochets and continues at the wall. They do not respawn.

Due to their slow speed and nonexistent threat due to their lack of contact damage, they are very good for gathering SOUL with the Dream Nail . This is especially useful for preparing for the Failed Champion boss fight.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Found in the Forgotten Crossroads , behind a breakable wall above False Knight's arena. They appear after False Knight has been defeated, but the room must be re-entered for them to spawn. Requires Mantis Claw to reach.

Trivia

The Maggot is one of the few enemies that never respawns. The False Knight is actually a maggot that stole Hegemol's armoured shell in order to protect his siblings. Upon death, Maggots emit an Infection cloud, despite not showing any hostility as Infected creatures do.
