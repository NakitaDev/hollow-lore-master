# Spiny Husk

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Spiny_Husk

Spiny Husks are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are husks whose bodies are overgrown with thorny vegetation who attack by firing sharp poisonous spines.

Behaviour and Tactics

It launches five spikes outwards in a dome formation when it is approached. Since there is a delay between launching spikes, it is advised to move in to trigger the launch, move away to avoid the spikes, and then go back in to deal damage.

Its body also sprouts spikes after death that deal contact damage; they can be destroyed by attacking the corpse. This does not happen if the Spiny Husk is killed with Spells .

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

The Spiny Husks are only found in the Queen's Gardens .

Trivia

Striking its corpse gives SOUL . The design for the Spiny Husk seems to be a modified version of the Husk Bully . The Spiny Husk corpses cannot be damaged by the Defender's Crest and Spore Shroom Charms .
