# Bluggsac

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Bluggsac

Bluggsacs are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are placid clusters of fluid sacs which occasionally produce a foul-smelling egg.

Behaviour and Tactics

Passive and stationary, it does not retaliate when attacked, nor does it deal contact damage. Drops a Rancid Egg upon death.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

They can be found scattered throughout Hallownest , with seemingly no pattern or main area of appearance.

1 in Blue Lake 2 in Crystal Peak 2 in Deepnest 1 in Kingdom's Edge 1 in Queen's Gardens 3 in Royal Waterways

Gallery

Trivia

Bluggsacs are a source of infinite SOUL as they can be hit with the Dream Nail repeatedly. They are not affected by environmental hazards and explosions. The Bluggsac is one of the few enemies that never respawns.
