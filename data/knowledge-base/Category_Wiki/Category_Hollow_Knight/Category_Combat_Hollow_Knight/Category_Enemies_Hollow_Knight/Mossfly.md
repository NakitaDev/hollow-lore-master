# Mossfly

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Mossfly

Mossflies are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are flying creatures who appear to be cousins of the Mosscreep , similarly covering themselves in leaves.

Behaviour and Tactics

Flies around aimlessly until engaged, then drifts slowly towards the Knight . When jumping to attack, remember to watch out for thorns below when falling.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Found mainly in the Queen's Gardens , they can also be spotted near the Overgrown Mound .

Gallery

Trivia

The Mossfly is one of the few enemies that respawns upon leaving and re-entering the room.
