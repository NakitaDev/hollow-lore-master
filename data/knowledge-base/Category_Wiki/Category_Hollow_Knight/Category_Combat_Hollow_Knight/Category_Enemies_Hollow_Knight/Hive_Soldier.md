# Hive Soldier

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Hive_Soldier

Hive Soldiers are enemies in Hollow Knight . These bee warriors spin their compound stingers to attack intruders.

Behaviour and Tactics

Hovers about passively until engaged. To attack, they reveal a massive, drill-like stinger and charge at the Knight after a few seconds, somewhat tracking their movement. Once impacting the ground, they remain stuck for one second, becoming vulnerable to attacks. Simply dodge their charge and hit them while they are stuck before backing off and repeating the process.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Found within the Hive .

Trivia

With the Hiveblood Charm equipped, they do not attack the Knight nor deal damage on contact. However, they still retaliate when attacked.
