# Pilflip

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Pilflip

Pilflips are enemies in Hollow Knight . They have antenna-like appendages on their back which function as limbs when they are flipped over.

Behaviour and Tactics

Patrols its territory without regard to the Knight . Upon being hit, it flips onto its back, changes direction, and begins to chase the Knight while hopping. Upon hitting it again, it changes direction and reverts back to patrolling.

If left off-screen for long enough, it reverts back to crawling.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

They are only found in the central part of the Royal Waterways .

Trivia

Its name is a palindrome (read the same both forwards and backwards). It is one of the few enemies that respawns upon leaving and re-entering the room.
