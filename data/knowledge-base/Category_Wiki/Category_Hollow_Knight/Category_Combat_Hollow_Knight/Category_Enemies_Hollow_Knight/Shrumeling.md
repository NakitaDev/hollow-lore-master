# Shrumeling

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Shrumeling

Shrumelings are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are defenceless young fungal creatures who spend most of their time in the ground, blindly running away when threatened.

Behaviour and Tactics

Stationary, but flees once approached. Does not have any attacks, deals no contact damage and dies in one hit. A useful source of free SOUL .

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Shrumelings can be found in the Fungal Wastes .
