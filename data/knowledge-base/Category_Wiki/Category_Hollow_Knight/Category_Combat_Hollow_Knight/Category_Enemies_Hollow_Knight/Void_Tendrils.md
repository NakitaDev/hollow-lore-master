# Void Tendrils

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Void_Tendrils

Void Tendrils are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are fragments of Void shaped like tendrils which lash out at intruders to push them away or pull them under.

Behaviour and Tactics

Void Tendrils emerge from pits of Void, reaching up and flailing if the Knight gets close. They deal contact damage and cannot be harmed, but are disabled when hit by the lighthouse's light or when Void Heart is active.

Location

They are only found in the Abyss , in pools of Void and in the sea of Void. The Void Tendrils' Journal entry is found within a secret area to the right of the lighthouse in the Abyss, blocked by a Shade Gate and accessed through a passage in the ceiling.

Trivia

Void Tendrils no longer appear after obtaining the Void Heart Charm . Void Tendrils cannot be read by the Dream Nail . Grimmchild and the Weaverlings spawned by the Weaversong Charm attack the tendrils, dealing no damage. When equipped with Grubsong , Weaversong will be able to extract SOUL from the tendrils, allowing for an infinite amount of SOUL to be obtained.
