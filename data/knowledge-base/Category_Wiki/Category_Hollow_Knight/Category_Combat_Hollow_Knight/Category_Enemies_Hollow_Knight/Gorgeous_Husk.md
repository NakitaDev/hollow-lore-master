# Gorgeous Husk

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Gorgeous_Husk

The Gorgeous Husk is an enemy in Hollow Knight . Its body is covered in a shining metal, and it still retains the instinct to collect Geo even after death.

Behaviour and Tactics

Meanders back and forth and is passive until attacked, though it is still harmful on contact. Once disturbed, it attacks just like the Gluttonous Husk , hopping at the Knight once in range. However, it is bigger and has much more health, making it slightly more challenging to beat.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

It is located in the northernmost room to the east of the Hollow Knight Fountain in the City of Tears . To reach it, travel east when entering the room in a secret chamber. There is a hidden step that is easy to miss as the Knight has to jump to enter the hidden room.

Trivia

The Gorgeous Husk drops more Geo than any regular enemy in the game. Only one Gorgeous Husk exists; it is one of the few enemies that never respawns.
