# Goam

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Goam

Goams are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are worm-like, thickly armoured creatures who burrowed tunnels through the walls of the caverns.

Behaviour and Tactics

Emerges from its hole quickly and then immediately rushes back in. It cannot be damaged or killed.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

They are mainly found in the Forgotten Crossroads , however, some can be found in Deepnest , at the area in which the Sharp Shadow Charm is collected. The Goam's Journal entry is found in the Forgotten Crossroads, obtained by inspecting a fossilised Goam. It is found in a hidden area to the right of the Fungal Wastes entrance. Requires the Desolate Dive / Descending Dark Spell to access.

Trivia

Other enemies die instantly when touched by a Goam. Despite being invincible, they have 91 health in the game's data. Although a challenging task due to their large size and speed, it is possible to Nail-bounce on their thick hides.
