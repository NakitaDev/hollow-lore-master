# Carver Hatcher

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Carver_Hatcher

Carver Hatchers are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are flying creatures who spawn Dirtcarvers when prey is nearby.

Behaviour and Tactics

Spawns Dirtcarvers while hovering above the Knight , tries to back away and evade when the Knight gets close.

It is best to take care of this enemy first in a fight, as otherwise, it continues to spawn enemies and makes the fight more difficult.

After spawning five Dirtcarvers, it starts swooping and chasing the Knight to bite them. While doing so it starts to emit a gurgling sound.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Carver Hatchers can only be spotted in the north part of Deepnest , where the Failed Tramway is located.
