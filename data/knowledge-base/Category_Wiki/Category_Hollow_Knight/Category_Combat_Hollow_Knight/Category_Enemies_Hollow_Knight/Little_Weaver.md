# Little Weaver

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Little_Weaver

Little Weavers are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are small Weavers who were part of the spider tribe of Deepnest .

Behaviour and Tactics

Hides in the shadows before emitting a screech and suddenly appearing. It slowly crawls along silk in the background and foreground (that cannot be interacted with) towards the Knight , dealing contact damage.

They tend to appear in places where there is a significant amount of spiderwebs.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

They can be found very commonly in the west portion of Deepnest, near the Distant Village .
