# Gulka

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Gulka

Gulkas are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are plant-like beings who spit venom-filled spikey balls at intruders. The venom has certain enjoyable effects when it is consumed.

Behaviour and Tactics

Shoots spiked balls in the Knight's direction. These balls arc very slightly with gravity.

When approached, it retracts into the wall or ceiling it has grown on, making it hard to kill. To damage it, increase the the Knight's range with a Charm such as Longnail , Mark of Pride , or Grubberfly's Elegy , use a Nail Art such as Dash Slash or  a Spell such as Vengeful Spirit / Shade Soul , or reflect the spiky balls it spits back at it by striking them with the Nail .

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Found in large quantities in Greenpath , to the west of the Stone Sanctuary .

Trivia

If the projectile it spits runs into a wall, it bounces about harmlessly for a few seconds before splitting open and disappearing. The Gulka is one of the few enemies that respawns upon leaving and re-entering the room.
