# Shardmite

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Shardmite

Shardmites are enemies in Hollow Knight . Formerly used in Crystal Peak's mining operations, these creatures now wander around on walls, feeding on crystal dust.

Behaviour and Tactics

Crawls up and down on vertical surfaces. Upon approaching it, it sprouts long crystal spikes from its shell. While those spikes are extended, it is invulnerable. By attacking quickly, it can be taken off guard and killed before it sprouts its spikes.

It is also possible to Nail-bounce off its spikes by down-slashing. With repeated down-slashing, the Shardmite retracts its spikes briefly before sprouting them again, giving the Knight a chance to get in a hit between spikes while keeping distance.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

They can only be found in Crystal Peak , mostly across the northeast part.
