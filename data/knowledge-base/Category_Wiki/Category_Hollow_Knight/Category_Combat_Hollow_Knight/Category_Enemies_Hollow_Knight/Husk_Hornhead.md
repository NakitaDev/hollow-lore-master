# Husk Hornhead

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Husk_Hornhead

Husk Hornheads are enemies in Hollow Knight . They use their horn to attack any creatures not stricken by the Infection .

Behaviour and Tactics

Lowers its head and charges at the Knight on sight but is otherwise rather weak.

Stand still and hit as it charges, or simply jump over it while down-slashing mid-charge.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

They can mainly be found throughout the Forgotten Crossroads , the City of Tears , and Deepnest . They can also be spotted in Greenpath , the Fungal Wastes , the Howling Cliffs , and the Royal Waterways .

Trivia

In a Kickstarter update , it was referred to as " Endemons Sapien (Infected) ":

"These vicious, mindless ghouls were once ordinary bugs like you and me. Something below the surface has burnt away their souls and filled them with an aimless rage."

In the pre-release A Weird and Wonderful World trailer, a Husk Hornhead is shown without its horn. A modified version of this original design is used as one of the forms of the Fungified Husk .
