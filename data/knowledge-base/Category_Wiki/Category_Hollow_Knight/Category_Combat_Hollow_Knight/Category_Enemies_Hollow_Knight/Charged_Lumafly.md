# Charged Lumafly

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Charged_Lumafly

Charged Lumaflies are enemies in Hollow Knight . They cluster together in small swarms, building up static charges.

Behaviour and Tactics

Generates damaging electricity at regular 2-second intervals and cannot be killed.

Location

They are found in abundance in Fog Canyon , but they can also be spotted in Greenpath , in the area where the Thorns of Agony Charm is collected.

Their Journal entry is found inside the Teacher's Archives in Fog Canyon , where they can be inspected in a tank.
