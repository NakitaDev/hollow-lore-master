# Husk Miner

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Husk_Miner

Husk Miners are enemies in Hollow Knight . Even after being overcome by the Infection , these reanimated corpses continue to mine the crystals in Crystal Peak .

Behaviour and Tactics

Mines listlessly until approached. Upon being disturbed, an orange dot, reminiscent of those seen in the eyes of other Infected husks, can be seen within its eyes, which otherwise would have appeared uninfected.

When hostile it runs feverishly back and forth until the Knight is within its sights. It attacks with a swing of its claw-pick if the Knight is in close range or by throwing it in a parabolic arc if the Knight is farther away.

The claw-picks can be reflected with the Nail and deal 5 damage. The best strategy is to simply avoid the first toss as the claw-pick damage is minimal and kill the Husk Miner before it can prepare another attack.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

The Husk Miner can only be found in Crystal Peak.
