# Furious Vengefly

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Furious_Vengefly

Furious Vengeflies are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are enormous Vengeflies whose bodies were distorted by continued Infection . The blisters around their wings appear to give them improved flight.

Behaviour and Tactics

Hovers in place. Flies towards the Knight in a zig-zag, up and down pattern when they get in range, emitting a screeching sound. A few seconds after being killed, its corpse explodes. While its regular attack deals one Mask of damage, the explosion deals two.

The Knight's standard Nail attack is enough for this enemy. Simply attack continuously to keep it out of range, then make sure to avoid the corpse after it is killed.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Furious Vengeflies can be commonly found in the Forgotten Crossroads , only after it becomes Infected . They replace the positions of most regular Vengeflies. They also appear in the 3rd wave of the Trial of the Fool at the Colosseum of Fools .
