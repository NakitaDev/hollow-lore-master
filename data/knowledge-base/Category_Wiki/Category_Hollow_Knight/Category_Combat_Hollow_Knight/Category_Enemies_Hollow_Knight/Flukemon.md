# Flukemon

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Flukemon

Flukemons are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are earthbound Flukes who split into two living halves after being defeated.

Behaviour and Tactics

Meanders back and forth, emitting a suckling sound to signal its presence. Once it spots the Knight , it rushes forward at them, dealing damage on contact. Even if it is blocked by a wall, it is hardly discouraged and keeps rushing towards the Knight. Can also detect when the Knight is above or below, even when not provoked and in this case, it stops wandering.

Shortly after its death, the top half reanimates. It emits a scream before flying at the Knight. The bottom half reanimates shortly after, making slobbering noises and running quickly at the Knight. It can run up walls and, due to its speed and momentum, attacking it early in the game can be risky - if not killed in one hit, there is a good chance that it runs back faster than a second swing, therefore it is safer to down-strike it by jumping over its head.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Flukemons can be found in the Royal Waterways , mostly in the central portion.

Trivia

The individual halves of the Flukemon cannot be Dream Nailed . The individual halves of the Flukemon cannot be damaged by the Defender's Crest and Spore Shroom Charms . If the Flukemon runs into water it has a unique animation where it cries out while slowly sinking into the water and eventually drowning.
