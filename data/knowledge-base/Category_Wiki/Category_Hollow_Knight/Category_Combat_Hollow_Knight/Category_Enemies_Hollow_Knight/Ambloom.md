# Ambloom

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Ambloom

Amblooms are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are simple-minded crawling mushrooms who do not attack directly.

Behaviour and Tactics

Meanders back and forth passively, but deals contact damage. It crawls about rather quickly, and can often be partially obscured by foreground environments.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Amblooms are found only in the Fungal Wastes , with four in the Fungal Core .

Gallery

Trivia

The Ambloom is one of the few enemies that respawns upon leaving and re-entering the room.
