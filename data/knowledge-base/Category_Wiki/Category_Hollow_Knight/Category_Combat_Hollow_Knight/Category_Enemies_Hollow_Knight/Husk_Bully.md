# Husk Bully

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Husk_Bully

Husk Bullies are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are walking corpses who attack anyone they can find who has not been Infected .

Behaviour and Tactics

Meanders back and forth passively until the Knight comes within range. It then hurls its body a short distance at the Knight. Because of its slow attack sequence and predictable move set, it is very easily dealt with and only poses a threat to the Knight when found in conjunction with other enemies.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Although found mostly in the Forgotten Crossroads , they can also be spotted in the City of Tears , Greenpath , the Howling Cliffs , and the Royal Waterways , much like their hornhead variant .
