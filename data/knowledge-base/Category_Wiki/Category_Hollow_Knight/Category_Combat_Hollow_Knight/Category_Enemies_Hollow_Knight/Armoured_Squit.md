# Armoured Squit

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Armoured_Squit

Armoured Squits are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are descendants of the Squit , bred exclusively for battle in the Colosseum of Fools .

Behaviour and Tactics

Behaves exactly like regular Squits, but with a health boost. It remains stationary until the Knight comes within range. Once engaged, it hovers a short distance away before flying directly at the Knight with its sharp proboscis raised. As it does not change direction during this charge, jumping or dashing over/through it is enough to avoid this attack.

Attacking an Armoured Squit mid-lunge changes its trajectory in the direction it is hit. They are most dangerous when grouped with other enemies, so it is best to take them out with one single hit of a Nail Art or Spell .

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Armoured Squits can be found in the Colosseum of Fools, in the Trial of the Conqueror and in the Trial of the Fool . Additionally, they can be spawned by the Collector in the Hall of Gods on Ascended difficulty or above, and in the Pantheon of Hallownest .
