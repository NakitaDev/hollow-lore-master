# Aspid Hunter

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Aspid_Hunter

Aspid Hunters are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are the males of the Aspid species. Aspid Hunters usually hunt in packs and spit corrosive liquid at prey from a distance. A more powerful variant called Primal Aspids can be found in Kingdom's Edge .

Behaviour and Tactics

Aimlessly hovers around until approached by the Knight . It attacks from a distance by spitting blobs of corrosive liquid.

It is best to get in close and strike as soon as possible. They can easily be heard from a distance by the whirring sound their wings make.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Aspid Hunters can be found in the Forgotten Crossroads (mainly the area where the Glowing Womb Charm is collected) and in the west side of the Ancient Basin Tram tunnel . They can also be spawned by the Collector .

Trivia

The Aspid Hunter is one of the few enemies that respawns upon leaving and re-entering the room. In a Kickstarter update , it was referred to as "Bulbous Hummingnit (Male)" :

"The male of this species is extremely aggressive, and will spit corrosive balls of venom at prey and intruders alike. Especially dangerous when encountered in packs."

Pre-release Aspid Hunters featured a reddish venom sack opposed to bright orange.
