# Sturdy Fool

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Sturdy_Fool

Sturdy Fools are enemies in Hollow Knight . These warriors fight in the Colosseum of Fools using their crescent-shaped blades.

Behaviour and Tactics

Sturdy Fools have two attacks:

Slash : Lunges and swipes at the Knight when in close range. Weapon Toss : Tosses its weapon in an arc at the Knight.

When they attack in melee range, simply jump over them and down-strike them for an easy hit. Hitting the blade back at the Sturdy Fool or any other enemy does 10 damage.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

This enemy is only found in the Colosseum of Fools, in the Trial of the Fool and the Trial of the Warrior .

Trivia

A non-aggressive Sturdy Fool can be found bathing, splashing it causes it to raise its hand and grunt. Using the Dream Nail on this Sturdy Fool gives unique dialogue.
