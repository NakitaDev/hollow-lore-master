# Shadow Creeper

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Shadow_Creeper

Shadow Creepers are enemies in Hollow Knight . They are possibly more primal relatives of the Crawlids , found only in deep, dark places.

Behaviour and Tactics

Meanders back and forth passively, sometimes vertically crawling on walls, with no regard to the Knight . It behaves exactly like the basic Crawlid, but can climb walls and has more health.

It also does not take any knockback from the Nail or Dream Nail .

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Shadow Creepers are found in Ancient Basin , mainly in the central portion. They can also be found in the Abyss , serving as platforming section obstacles in the entrance.
