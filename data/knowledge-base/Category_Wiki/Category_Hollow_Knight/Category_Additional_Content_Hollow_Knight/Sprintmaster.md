# Sprintmaster

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Sprintmaster

(For the NPC in Hollow Knight: Silksong , see Sprintmaster Swift )

Sprintmaster is a Charm in Hollow Knight . It increases run speed by ~20%.

Usefulness

The additional speed is only applied while the Knight is grounded and not impeded by walk-zones or liquid such as acid or water.

The Sprintmaster Charm can be useful when fighting quick, agile bosses, such as Hornet .

How to Acquire

Bought from Sly in Dirtmouth after acquiring the Shopkeeper's Key for 400. Shop description:

You like it? It's a recent addition to my collection. Handsome thing, isn't it? Holding it lets you run around quickly and tirelessly, as though you were young again! Or, erm, so I'm told.
