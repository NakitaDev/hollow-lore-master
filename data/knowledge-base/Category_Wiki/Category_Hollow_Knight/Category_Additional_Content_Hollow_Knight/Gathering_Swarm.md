# Gathering Swarm

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Gathering_Swarm

Gathering Swarm is a Charm in Hollow Knight . It spawns a swarm that collects dropped Geo .

Usefulness

This Charm is primarily used to grind Geo more easily. It is useful for collecting Geo that falls into spikes, thorns, and hard to reach places. If the area is exited before all Geo has been collected, it is lost (Geo carried by the swarm that has not yet reached the Knight is not lost).

Gathering Swarm does not collect Geo that falls into acid, Geo that is dropped by Grubfather , or Geo rewarded for completing a Trial in the Colosseum of Fools .

How to Acquire

Bought from Sly in Dirtmouth for 300.

Shop description:

Do you find yourself leaving a lot of Geo behind as you hurry through the caverns? This charm will make sure that any loose change finds its way back to you.
