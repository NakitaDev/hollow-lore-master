# Grimmsteed

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Grimmsteed

The Grimmsteeds are two NPCs in Hollow Knight . They appear together with the Grimm Troupe in Dirtmouth .

Lore

The Grimmsteeds are two faithful bugs who are part of the Grimm Troupe. They are possibly the ones who pull the Troupe's caravan. Due to their visual similarities, they appear to be of the same species as Willoh .

The Grimmsteeds sit silently in front of Grimm's tent, waiting for the performance to begin.

In-game events

The Grimmsteeds arrive in Dirtmouth after the Nightmare Lantern in the Howling Cliffs has been lit. They do not speak, but their thoughts can be read with the Dream Nail . They disappear after Nightmare King Grimm has been defeated or the Troupe is banished.

Location

Only two Grimmsteeds exist, and both are located in Dirtmouth after the Grimm Troupe has been summoned. They are outside the main tent.
