# Quick Slash

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Quick_Slash

Quick Slash is a Charm in Hollow Knight . It allows for more rapid Nail strikes.

Usefulness

Quick Slash can increase total damage output while slashing with the Nail. This Charm is more effective than Fragile Strength / Unbreakable Strength in most situations, as it not only increases the rate at which the Nail deals damage but also how quickly it accumulates SOUL .

How to Acquire

Located in Kingdom's Edge , on a massive anvil in a hidden room in front of a massive corpse known as an " Ancient Nailsmith ".

Finding it requires travelling to the very east end of Kingdom's Edge past Oro's hut and locating a breakable floor next to his training dummy. After traversing down a few levels, and looping up the west end of the section, there is a small tunnel located within a wall leading to the Charm.

Note that there is spike-filled room with a Great Hopper. The Great Hopper makes it immensely difficult to cross through the spikes.
Take out any primal aspids before entering this room or else they will make things even more tough.
