# Weaversong

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Weaversong

Weaversong is a Charm in Hollow Knight . It summons 3 Weaverlings that attack enemies , dealing 3 damage per hit.

Usefulness

The Weaverlings attack automatically, making it a bit easier to deal damage to enemies that might be out of range.
If a Weaverling falls out of the world or loses track of the Knight , a new one will spawn after a few moments.

While wearing this Charm, Midwife has a new piece of dialogue and Dream Nail dialogue.

How to Acquire

Found in the upper part of Weavers' Den .

Trivia

If the Knight sits on a bench, the summoned Weaverlings will sit down on the ground next to them. The animation for a Weaverling spawning shows it being woven out of thread. In the Lifeblood update, Weaverling damage was increased from 2 to 3, and both the Grubsong and Sprintmaster interactions were added. However, the number of Weaverlings summoned was decreased by 1, from 4 to 3. This charm is a creation of the Weavers . If the Knight has either the Grimmchild or Carefree Melody Charm equipped, the Weaverlings try to attack Millibelle at her bank in Fog Canyon . This deals no damage, but prevents the Knight from using her banking service, unless Grimmchild is in its first Phase.
