# Winged Zoteling

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Winged_Zoteling

Winged Zotelings are enemies in Hollow Knight , introduced in the Hidden Dreams update. They are flying figments of Bretta's obsessed mind.

Behaviour and Tactics

Slowly flies towards the Knight after spawning.

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

Winged Zotelings are spawned by Grey Prince Zote . The boss must be defeated to earn the Winged Zoteling's Journal entry , but the Winged Zoteling does not have to actually appear.

They are also present in the Eternal Ordeal .
