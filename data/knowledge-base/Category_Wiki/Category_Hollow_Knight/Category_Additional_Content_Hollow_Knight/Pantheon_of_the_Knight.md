# Pantheon of the Knight

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Pantheon_of_the_Knight

The Pantheon of the Knight is the fourth Pantheon in Godhome . The previous Pantheon is the Pantheon of the Sage , and the next is the Pantheon of Hallownest . Access to this Pantheon is restricted until the previous three Pantheons have been completed and the necessary bosses have been defeated.

Trivia

Defeating Failed Champion , Soul Tyrant , Lost Kin and White Defender is not required to access this Pantheon. When this Pantheon is completed for the first time, a cutscene is shown right after defeating Pure Vessel which involves the Hollow Knight , the Knight , and numerous other Vessels .
