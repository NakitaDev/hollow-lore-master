# Steady Body

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Steady_Body

Steady Body is a Charm in Hollow Knight . It removes horizontal knockback from Nail hits.

Usefulness

In addition to removing knockback from hitting enemies , spikes, and walls, it also prevents the Knight from being knocked left or right when jumping on purple mushrooms without using the Nail. It does not remove the knockback from hitting purple mushrooms horizontally or any vertical knockback, such as from Nail-bouncing .

How to Acquire

Bought from Salubra for 120 in the Forgotten Crossroads .

Shop description:

I love the simple design on this little fellow! Very classy! It's made out of a nice, solid, heavy material too. Wear it and you'll be able to stay firmly in place when swinging your little nail around.
