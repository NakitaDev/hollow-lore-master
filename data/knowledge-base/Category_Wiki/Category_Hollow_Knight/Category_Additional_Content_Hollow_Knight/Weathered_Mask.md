# Weathered Mask

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Weathered_Mask

The Weathered Mask is a Journal entry in Hollow Knight . It is a mask with a crest that used to belong to the Godseekers in the Land of Storms .

How to Acquire

The Weathered Mask is found within the Land of Storms, a secret area of Godhome . To access this area, all Pantheons need to be completed with all Bindings . The Bindings can be done any number at a time.
