# Pantheons

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Pantheons

Pantheons are a mechanic added to Godhome . They feature a select set of bosses to combat before reaching the "Peak" of each Pantheon and facing the unique final boss of each challenge. Most bosses, with the exception of the final bosses, need to be defeated outside Godhome first before the Pantheon can be accessed that contains those bosses.

Completing the first three Pantheons also unlocks Godseeker Mode .

Trivia

When entering or exiting a Pantheon, Masks are fully restored, and Lifeblood Masks that come from Lifeseeds are removed. Activating the Dreamgate during any of the Pantheons causes the Knight to leave the arena and wake up in front of the respective entrance. During the Nintendo Treehouse Live during E3 2018 , a Pantheon was shown off by the name of "Pantheon of Stone" with the description of "Seek the Gods of nail and shell". The roster of Bosses included in this Pantheon are, in order: Vengefly King Dung Defender Mega Moss Charger ( Massive Moss Charger ) Hornet Paintmaster Sheo Due to a bug, within the Pantheon of Hallownest, the music will sometimes fail to play.
