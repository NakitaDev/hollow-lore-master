# Volatile Zoteling

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Volatile_Zoteling

Volatile Zotelings are enemies in Hollow Knight , introduced in the Hidden Dreams update. They are figments of Bretta's obsessed mind that quickly explode after coming into existence.

Behaviour and Tactics

Floats in the air before exploding, dealing two Masks of damage. They appear in groups when fighting Grey Prince Zote and individually during the Eternal Ordeal .

Dream Nail Dialogue

Location

This enemy is spawned by Grey Prince Zote . They appear in Battle 3 and onwards. To complete the entry for the Hunter's Journal , only Grey Prince Zote needs to be beaten in Battle 3 regardless of whether this enemy appears.

Volatile Zotelings are also present in the Eternal Ordeal .
