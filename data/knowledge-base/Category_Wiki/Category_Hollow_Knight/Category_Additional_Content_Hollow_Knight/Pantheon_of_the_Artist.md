# Pantheon of the Artist

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Pantheon_of_the_Artist

The Pantheon of the Artist is the second Pantheon in Godhome . The previous Pantheon is the Pantheon of the Master , and the next is the Pantheon of the Sage .
