# Lifeblood Heart

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Lifeblood_Heart

Lifeblood Heart is a Charm in Hollow Knight . It adds 2 Lifeblood Masks when resting at a Bench .

Usefulness

As with Lifeblood Core , this Charm is useful if the Knight focuses on Spells , as it allows them to use more of their SOUL on casting rather than healing. The lower cost is useful for saving Notches, but 2 Lifeblood Masks are not very significant compared to Lifeblood Core's four Masks or even healing Charms like Quick Focus and Hiveblood .

How to Acquire

Bought from Salubra in the Forgotten Crossroads for 250.

Shop description:

Have you ever drunk that bright blue liquid, 'lifeblood'? It's a bit of a taboo, but it makes you feel much healthier doesn't it? This charm seeps lifeblood and will certainly improve your constitution!
