# Sheo

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Sheo

Nailmaster Sheo is one of three Nailmasters in Hollow Knight . Although retired, he teaches the Great Slash Nail Art to those seeking him. Sheo also appears as a boss as Paintmaster Sheo .

Lore

Sheo learnt the Nail Arts from the Great Nailsage Sly , along with his brothers Oro and Mato . Despite his aloof and kind demeanour, he was the most skilled disciple, driven solely by his desire for strength and the mastering of his skills. Yet, this passion faded over time, as he desired to explore new forms of arts that could move him. He still had a good relationship with both of his quarrelling brothers and had respect for his master.

After some time, Sheo hung up his nail to become an artist. He set a hut in a far corner of Greenpath where he experiments various forms of creative art in peace. He hopes to be able to show how far he has come in his craft to his old teacher.

In-game events

Sheo can be found in his hut in Greenpath. When the Knight comes by, the retired Nailmaster is painting an image of a bug shell. Facing the Knight's disinterest in his arts, Sheo finally concedes in teaching them his signature move, the Great Slash. He, however, chooses to do so using only his brush, explaining that it is the same as a nail to him.

After his lesson, Sheo invites the Knight to stay and paint with him whenever they visit. He recognizes when the Knight has learnt a new Nail Art from his brothers, and hopes that it brought happiness to them both. He also takes notices of the Nailsage's acknowledgement of the Knight as Nailmaster. This moves him, but not enough to make him stray from his current path of artist.

If the Nailsmith was spared after forging the Pure Nail , he eventually finds his way to Sheo's hut. Seeing the Nailsmith distraught about his own life, they talk and discover their common love of creation and honing a skill. From this point, Sheo starts teaching the Nailsmith whichever art he experimented in so far. They eventually move on to crafting models of the Five Great Knights of Hallownest . Sheo remembers them well, while mentioning Hegemol was his favourite. The achievement name, Happy Couple , implies Sheo and the Nailsmith are in a romantic relationship.

Sheo's mind is summoned to Godhome by the Godseeker's ritual. However he appears there not as the Nailmaster he was, but as the Paintmaster he became. As such, he battles the Knight with his brush and paints, expressing formidable strength in doing so, and bows to them once defeated. While in Godhome, Sheo also takes inspiration from his host's motivation and quest for his own pursuit of arts. Paintmaster Sheo is mainly fought at the peak of the Pantheon of the Artist and later within the Pantheon of Hallownest .
