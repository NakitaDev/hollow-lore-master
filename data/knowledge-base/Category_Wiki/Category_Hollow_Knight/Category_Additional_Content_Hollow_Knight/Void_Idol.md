# Void Idol

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Void_Idol

The Void Idol is a Journal entry in Hollow Knight . It is an artifact that originates from the Ancient Civilisation which contains Void .

How to Acquire

The Void Idol is found within the Hall of Gods by inspecting the Knight 's statue after beating all bosses on at least one difficulty. Beating all bosses subsequently on Ascended and Radiant each change the idol and its description upon inspecting the statue again.
