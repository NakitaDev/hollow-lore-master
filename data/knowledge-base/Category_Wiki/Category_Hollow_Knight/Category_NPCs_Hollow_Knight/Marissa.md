# Marissa

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Marissa

Marissa is a spirit in Hollow Knight . She was a songstress in the Pleasure House in the City of Tears .

Lore

Marissa was a songstress of some renown in Hallownest's capital. She performed in the Pleasure House for enrapt crowds, with a voice that was described as being able to ease burdens and still troubled minds. When the Infection spread across Hallownest, Marissa's audiences eventually stopped coming to her performances.

At some point, Marissa passed away. Her spirit remained on the stage where she used to perform. She continued to sing here even though nobody was left to listen.

In-game events

If the Knight has the Dream Nail , Marissa can be heard singing on her stage in the Pleasure House. She appears on the stage when the Knight approaches it. After explaining her backstory to the Knight, she asks if they would like to listen to her sing.

It is possible for the Knight to sit on the stage to listen to Marissa sing. Even though only the Knight is there to hear her, Marissa enjoys having an audience again.

Like all other spirits, the Dream Nail can be used on her, giving 1 Essence .

Dialogue

Location

Marissa is located in the Pleasure House in the City of Tears. A Simple Key is required to unlock the entrance to the Pleasure House.
