# Bardoon

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Bardoon

Bardoon is an NPC in Hollow Knight . He provides lore on the Cast-Off Shell and the Infection .

Lore

Bardoon is an old caterpillar found sleeping in Kingdom's Edge . He climbed up to the top of Kingdom’s Edge to get away from bugs plagued by the Infection. He resists the light’s allure, which he explains offers unity but also reduces a bug to instinct alone.

Bardoon has knowledge of the Pale King's origins and the King's Brand . Though he knows about Wyrms , he is not one himself.

In-game events

His body can be seen in the background, twisting through the cliff face. Bardoon's tail is found southeast of his head; hitting it makes him chuckle. He does not mind it when his tail is struck.

Bardoon has new dialogue after the King's Brand has been acquired, as well as Void Heart .

Location

Bardoon's head can be found in the north part of Kingdom's Edge. His tail is found near the Lifeblood Cocoon in this area, behind a breakable wall.

Trivia

Bardoon is one of the few NPCs who can be "hit" with the Nail without taking damage. Bardoon is one of the few NPCs who is able to detect when they are being Dream Nailed , along with Moths , Snail Shamans and the White Lady . Concept art of Bardoon labels him as "Deepnest Caterpillar" , indicating that he was originally intended to be located in Deepnest . Kingdom's Edge used to be part of Deepnest. Deepnest was split in two after the removal of the Forest of Bones area. The east part was moved to the bottom right of the map and eventually became Kingdom's Edge. This can still be seen in Kingdom's Edge's file names, which start with "Deepnest_East".
