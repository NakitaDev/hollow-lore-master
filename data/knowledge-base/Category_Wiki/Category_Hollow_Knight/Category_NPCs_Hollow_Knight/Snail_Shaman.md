# Snail Shaman

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Snail_Shaman

Snail Shaman is an NPC in Hollow Knight . He gives the Vengeful Spirit Spell to the Knight in the Ancestral Mound .

Lore

Snail Shaman is a member of the snail shaman tribe . His relatives can be found around Hallownest , but since he is bound to the Ancestral Mound he is unable to visit them. He is therefore unaware of their fate.

While Snail Shaman can create Soul Spells such as Vengeful Spirit, he and the other snails are unable to make Spells like Abyss Shriek , having never been to the Abyss .

In-game events

Upon first encounter, the Shaman gifts the Vengeful Spirit Spell to the Knight. Accepting it results in the Knight waking up locked up in a small dungeon, tasked with killing an Elder Baldur within the Ancestral Mound.

The bodies of other snail shamans can be found throughout Hallownest. There is a crystallised one in the Crystallised Mound , one with overgrown flora in the Overgrown Mound and one in the Soul Sanctum that looks like it has been experimented on, as well as a snail sarcophagus where the Soul Eater Charm can be found.

Acquiring Spells from these snails causes Snail Shaman to comment on how these spells remind him of his various relatives.

Dialogue

Location

Snail Shaman is found in the Ancestral Mound in the Forgotten Crossroads . He can be accessed after defeating False Knight or breaking the wall on the left side of False Knight's boss room.
