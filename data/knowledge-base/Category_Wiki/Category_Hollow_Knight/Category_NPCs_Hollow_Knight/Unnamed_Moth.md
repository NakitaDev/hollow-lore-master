# Unnamed Moth

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Unnamed_Moth

Unnamed Moth is an NPC in Hollow Knight . They appear in the Dream Realm .

Lore

Unnamed Moth is a moth who only appears in the Dream Realm. They stand outside of the Shrine of Believers , commenting on the messages inside the Shrine.

In-game events

Unnamed Moth can be reached by using the Dream Nail on a moth statue in Spirits' Glade .

They note about the contents of the Shrine; which are quotes from Kickstarter backers displayed on tablets. Once all the tablets are destroyed Unnamed Moth has different dialogue.
