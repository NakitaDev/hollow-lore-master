# Thistlewind

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Thistlewind

Thistlewind is a moth spirit in Hollow Knight . They give information on the Moth Tribe and on Markoth .

Lore

Thistlewind was an explorer who travelled across Hallownest . Despite the moths being a passive species, they were one of the few moths who wielded a weapon. Eventually, Thistlewind died and was buried in Spirits' Glade .

In-game events

Thistlewind appears above their grave in Spirits' Glade when the Knight approaches. They explain that they have stopped travelling Hallownest and suspect that it has become a much more dangerous place. Thistlewind wonders what became of Markoth, who went to Kingdom's Edge to uncover a truth long forgotten.

Like all other spirits, the Dream Nail can be used on them, giving 1 Essence . This causes Revek to attack.

Dialogue

Location

Thistlewind is located in Spirits' Glade in the Resting Grounds .
