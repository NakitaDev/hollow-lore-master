# Little Fool

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Little_Fool

Little Fool is a Merchant in Hollow Knight . He collects the participation fees from contestants to the Colosseum of Fools .

Lore

Little Fool is a dedicated supporter of the Fools battling for glory in the Colosseum's Trials. He himself dreams of proving his strength in battle to Lord Fool .

In-game events

Little Fool is found chained up, hanging upside-down at the entrance of the Colosseum. Despite his predicament, he informs the Knight of the rules of the arena and takes payment to unlock each of the three Trials.

For 100, he unlocks the Trial of the Warrior For 450, he unlocks the Trial of the Conqueror For 800, he unlocks the Trial of the Fool

Dialogue

Location

Little Fool is located in the Colosseum of Fools, in the entrance room.
