# Arcane Egg

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Arcane_Egg

Arcane Eggs are jet black stone eggs originating from a civilisation that existed before the Kingdom of Hallownest was founded. According to Relic Seeker Lemm , each of these eggs contains knowledge and records within its various layers, although only the outer layer is accessible to Lemm. Other Relic Seekers claim that there is a will within these eggs, with each seeking its own bearer, but Lemm remains sceptical of those claims.

Use

Can be sold to Relic Seeker Lemm for 1200.

Shop description:

Ah! This seems like a simple egg, but it's actually a precious relic from before the birth of Hallownest! I'll pay you a small fortune for it. Please sell it to me!

How to Acquire

There are a total of 4 Arcane Eggs that can be obtained:

3 found in the Abyss 1 located below the room in a secret path where Lifeblood Core is found 1 directly west of where Shade Cloak is acquired 1 in the south part of the Birthplace (requires Kingsoul ) 1 given by the Seer in the Resting Grounds as a reward for 1,200 Essence
