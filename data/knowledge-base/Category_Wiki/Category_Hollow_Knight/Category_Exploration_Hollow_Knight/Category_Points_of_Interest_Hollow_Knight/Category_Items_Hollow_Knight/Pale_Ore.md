# Pale Ore

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Pale_Ore

Pale Ore is an Item in Hollow Knight .

Use

Pale Ore can be found in several places throughout Hallownest . Giving the Nailsmith Pale Ore along with a payment of Geo allows him to upgrade the Knight's Nail.

The first upgrade to the Nail requires no Pale Ore, the second requires one, the third requires two, and the final upgrade requires three Pale Ore, for a total of six.
