# Pharloom

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Pharloom

Pharloom is the location where Hollow Knight: Silksong takes place.

The kingdom of Pharloom is located in a distant and withered land. It is ruled and haunted by silk and song, the two powerful forces of that world. Like Hallownest in its golden age, Pharloom is dedicated to faith and pilgrimage, although the gilded pin is its traditional weapon and the silk spool its symbol. Pharloom is crowned by the shining Citadel , the final destination for all pilgrims, and the place where all the secrets of the haunting lie.
