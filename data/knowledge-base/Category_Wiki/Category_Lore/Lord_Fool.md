# Lord Fool

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Lord_Fool

Lord Fool was a bug who presumably oversaw the Trials at the Colosseum of Fools . While they appear to be long dead as of the events of Hollow Knight , their corpse still remains seated on the throne in the Colosseum as if observing the Trials.

Little is known about Lord Fool aside from their appearance, and the fact that they were powerful enough for the warriors of the Colosseum to respect them.

Little Fool does not seem aware of Lord Fool's demise . The audience in the arena is either also unaware, or most likely simply does not care.

Lore

Though not much is known about the Lord Fool, multiple community members have proposed theories about him. One of such theories supposes the Colosseum of Fools is actually a Wyrm corpse, similar to the Cast off Shell also in Kingdoms Edge . This theory ideates that the Lord Fool is a higher being form of the Colosseum, as The Pale King is to the Cast off Shell. Some even believe the colosseum is the legendary Blackwyrm hinted at through various dialogue. Evidence also comes from the way which the Lord Fool has void like eye sockets, which appear damaged similar to that of the Pale King. None of these theories have been confirmed, however, the nature of the Lord Fool is still debated.

Trivia
