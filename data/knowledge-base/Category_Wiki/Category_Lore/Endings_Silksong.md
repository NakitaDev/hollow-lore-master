# Endings (Silksong)

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Endings_(Silksong)

The following is a list of all endings that can be achieved in Hollow Knight: Silksong .

Ending 1: Weaver Queen

The Weaver Queen Ending is the standard conclusion to Hollow Knight: Silksong and will likely be the first ending most players see. In this version of events, Hornet defeats Grand Mother Silk at the Citadel and chooses to Bind her power. This transforms Hornet into a part-weaver, part-wyrm being, and her silk spreads across Pharloom, cocooning the kingdom and trapping its people. It’s suggested that Hornet now known as Weaver Queen is no longer Hornet as Grand Mother Silk’s power is too big for her to contain.

How to achieve this ending

After defeating Grand Mother Silk at the end of act 2, picking the bind option will achieve this ending.

Ending 2: Snared Silk

Similar to the Weaver Queen Ending, but instead of binding Grand Mother Silk, Hornet plays the Needolin. This summons the Soul Snare and creates a pool of Void below Grand Mother Silk. Void Tendrils begin to pull Grand Mother Silk into the Void, but Grand Mother Silk grabs Hornet and begins to crush her. Lace jumps in, cuts off Grand Mother Silk's arm, and stabs her in the chest, letting Hornet free. Grand Mother Silk and Lace get pulled into the Void together. A large blast of force echoes throughout the Citadel, destroying it. Outside the Citadel, black threads of writhing Void begin to emerge.

How to achieve this ending

After defeating Grand Mother Silk at the end of act 2, picking the Needolin option will achieve this ending.

Ending 3: Twisted Child

Similar to the Weaver Queen ending, Hornet defeats Grand Mother Silk while Hornet is infested with the Twisted Bud. Twisted roots come out of Hornet and wrap themselves around Grand Mother Silk. The screen goes black, and when the screen fades in again, Grand Mother Silk and Hornet have been turned to Twisted Wood, resting at the top of the Cradle. The screen zooms out as Grand Mother Silk's body breaks open, and when the screen goes black, an ominous growling sounds.

How to achieve this ending

Defeat Grand Mother Silk while infested with the Twisted Bud.

Ending 4: Sister of The Void

After defeating Lost Lace, Lace's body emerges in the center of the silk cocoon at the bottom of the Void Ocean. Hornet takes her body and with Grand Mother Silk's help, uses Silksoar to ascend the Void Ocean and escape. During this, the Everbloom disintegrates and Hornet and Lace are left drifting in the Void. As tendrils rush to attack them, an outside force stops them. The Shade Lord, better known as the Knight appears with the Siblings from the Abyss in Hallownest, and saves Hornet and Lace. Shade Lord pushes Hornet and Lace outside of the Void Ocean, and Hornet and Lace look at each other, Lace silently laughing.

How to achieve this ending

Defeat Lost Lace at the end of act 3.

Extra ending: Passing of the Age

After completing the Passing of The Age quest and getting any ending, a shot of the Surface appears on the screen. A small bug climbs towards the screen and looks up to see Mister Mushroom soaring up. The screen zooms in on him, then it goes black, the words "To Be Continued" appearing on the screen.

How to achieve this ending

Complete the Passing of The Age quest and achieve any ending.
