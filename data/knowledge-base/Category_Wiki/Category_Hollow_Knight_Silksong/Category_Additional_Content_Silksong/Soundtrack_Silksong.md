# Soundtrack (Silksong)

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Soundtrack_(Silksong)

The original soundtrack (or OST for short) of Hollow Knight: Silksong is being composed and performed by Christopher Larkin, and a few other artists. A sample track was first officially shared on the game's official website, and a sample album of two tracks was released on Bandcamp for Team Cherry's 2019 holiday sign off blog post.
