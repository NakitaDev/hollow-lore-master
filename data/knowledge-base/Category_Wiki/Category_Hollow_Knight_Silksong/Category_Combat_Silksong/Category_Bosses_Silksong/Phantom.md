# Phantom

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Phantom

Phantom is a boss in Hollow Knight: Silksong.  They are one of the two possible bosses required to enter Act 2, the other being Last Judge .

Phantom is a creature strung from silk, by Grand Mother Silk . Phantom and Lace consider each other siblings. Phantom plays the Exhaust Organ on the edge of Bilewater , expelling a mixture of steam and silk dregs into the surrounding air, which has transformed a small part of Sinner’s Road and Bilewater into The Mist .

Killing Phantom allows  Hornet to gain the Cross Stitch ability.
