# Clover Dancers

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Clover_Dancers

The Clover Dancers is a boss in Hollow Knight: Silksong.

The boss fight is in the memory of the Green Prince . You must use the Elegy of the Deep on him when he is in Verdania, which is only accessible in Act 3 and is located to the right of Craw Lake.

After you use the Elegy of the Deep, you enter Lost Verdania, the memory of what the Green Prince's land used to be. Once you collect 12 glowing orbs, you are able to enter the Green Prince's  home and fight the Clover Dancers. The fight is a 2v1, with you fighting the Green Prince and the memory of his unnamed partner. It is very similar to the Cogwork Dancers, though you cannot damage the Green Prince's partner.

The Green Prince and his partner were the two rulers of Verdania. As the Citadel grew, the two princes were worried that they would try to take their territory. So, the Green Prince's partner sacrificed himself to prevent their land from being taken. However, he was made into the model bug for the Cogwork Dancers. Verdania died out, so the Green Prince was locked into  Sinner's Road.
