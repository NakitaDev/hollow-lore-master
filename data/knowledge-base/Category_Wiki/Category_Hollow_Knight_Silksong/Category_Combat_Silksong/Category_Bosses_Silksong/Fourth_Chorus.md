# Fourth Chorus

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Fourth_Chorus

Fourth Chorus is a boss in Hollow Knight: Silksong . It appears in Deep Docks after completing the Flexile Spines Quest in Far Fields and receiving the Drifters Cloak cloak upgrade from The Seamstress. It's described as a metal automation that works in the Deep Docks.

In the first phase of the fight, it has two main attacks: it slams down vertically with one of its two top hands, or swipes horizontally across the screen with one of its bottom two hands. The vertical attack temporarily destroys the platform underneath it, so Hornet cannot stand on it. Falling into the lava there deals two masks of damage. The boss only takes damage from hits on its head, since the rest of its body is a sturdy metal.

Once Fourth Chorus has staggered, another attack is added, which causes lava balls to rain down from the ceiling. Coming into contact with one does one mask of damage.

In the final stage of the fight, which comes after 3 staggers, upward drafts will appear to the left and right of the battle platform, which is now slightly smaller. While it's possible to beat Fourth Chorus by attacking its head until it falls, it can also be defeated indirectly. This can be done when the upward drafts show up, by riding them to the top and hitting the magma blocks (one on each side) in the ceiling. This causes a Boulder to fall and crush the boss's head.
