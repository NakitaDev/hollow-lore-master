# Craggler

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Craggler

The Craggler is a mini-boss in Hollow Knight: Silksong. The thing is a sort of crab-walker on the ceiling, and spits acid doing double damage. It can be killed. Since, if you take the time to kill it, you may have gotten needle upgrades by that point, the hits would vary.

With a beginner nail it takes 40 hits.
