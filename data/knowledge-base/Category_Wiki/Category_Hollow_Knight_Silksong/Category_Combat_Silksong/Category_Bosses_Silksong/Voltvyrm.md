# Voltvyrm

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Voltvyrm

Voltvyrm is an optional boss in Hollow Knight: Silksong . It's located in the Voltnest

In-game events

By exploring the Sands of Karak, the player can enter a sub-area called the Voltnest. Eventually, they can encounter Voltvyrm in a room in the bottom left of the zone. Stepping into the room will cause the fight to start. When defeated, the player can claim the Volt filament on its corpse.

Behaviour and Tactics

Electric Spark : Voltvyrm will create pink circles that will expand and unleash electricity after a short time Electric Pillars : Pale pink pillars with glowing circles will appear. Electricity will then appear on the pillars after a short time.

Needolin dialogue

Voltvyrm does not respond to the Needolin.

Location

Voltvyrm can be found in Voltnest, at the top of the vertical central room.
