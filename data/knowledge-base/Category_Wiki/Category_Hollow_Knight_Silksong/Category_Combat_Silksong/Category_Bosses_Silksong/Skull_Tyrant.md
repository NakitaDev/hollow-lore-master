# Skull Tyrant

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Skull_Tyrant

The Skull Tyrant is a boss in Hollow Knight: Silksong. It makes its first appearance in The Marrow, the fight can be triggered by attacking it. It appears later in Bone Bottom, which is its final appearance.

Upon death, will drop the Crown Fragment .

Behavior and Tactics

The Skull Tyrant cannot be damaged from above, as hitting its head causes Hornet to bounce off of it.

The Skull Tyrant has three main attacks:

Charge : Skull Tyrant will appear to shuffle away right before rushing at Hornet.

Jump : Skull Tyrant will, after standing still for a moment, jump towards Hornet growling right before lunging.

Throw : Skull Tyrant will stand in place and slam the ground, this will cause rocks to fall downwards. Bright lights will appear where they will appear.

Skull Tyrant will appear on  the right side of the arena; Note that the arena can be left at any time, but will cause the boss to regain all its health upon returning to its arena.
