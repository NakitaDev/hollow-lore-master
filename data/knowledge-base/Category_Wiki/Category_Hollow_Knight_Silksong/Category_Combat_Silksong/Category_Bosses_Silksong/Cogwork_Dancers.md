# Cogwork Dancers

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Cogwork_Dancers

The Cogwork Dancers are a duo boss in Hollow Knight: Silksong . These bosses are found in the Cogwork Core of The Citadel . The Cogwork Dancers move in sync with each other, with white lines choreographing their movement. This boss has 4 phases.

Behavior

Phase 1: Both dancers will move at the same time in the direction of their white line. Occasionally they will create a circle in the middle which creates a barrage of slashes. They can also create a bunch of lines across the arena and cut across them rapidly.

Phase 2: Both dancers are much faster.

Phase 3: The dancers now move one at a time, out of sync.

Phase 4: After 1 dancer dies, the other one will become extremely slow. They will attempt to do their circle barrage attack, but without their partner, they simply float slowly to the ground.

Note: Prior to the start of each phase, the cogs in the background will wind up the dancers accordingly. Before the first phase, the cogs wind up normally. Before the second, they wind up noticeably faster. Before the third, they take turns winding up one at a time. Before the final phase, the remaining dancer’s cogs wind up slowly.
