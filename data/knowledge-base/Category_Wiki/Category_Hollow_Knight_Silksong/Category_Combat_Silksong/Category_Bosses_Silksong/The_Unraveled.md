# The Unraveled

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/The_Unraveled

The Unravelled is an optional Act 2 boss in Hollow Knight: Silksong. It drops a Silk Heart upon defeat.

The Unravelled is located in the Whitewardsection of the game, in order to fight it you will need to have unlocked the Ancestral Art; Clawline and acquired the Surgeon's Key.

Start from the Bench located in Whiteward, then proceed towards the west side of Whiteward to look for the Ornate Lock for which you may use the Surgeon's Key.

After the lock has been unlocked you may proceed all the way down until you reach the location of The Unravelled.
