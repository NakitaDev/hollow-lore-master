# Moorwing

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Moorwing

Location

Moorwing is an enemy and boss in Hollow Knight: Silksong , and can be found in the Greymoor area left of the Bellway station.

Attacks and Behaviour

The Moorwing has 3 different attacks in phase 1, those being a side dive, many quick slashes downwards and throwing a bone saw along the ground. In phase 2 it gains a few new moves including  throwing two bone saws in a circle downwards making you have to jump twice and the phase 1 bone saw move, but with two saws instead of just one. The Moorwing will also fall, so make sure you're not beneath it, or you will be dealt 2 masks of damage.

Appearance

Moorwing has brownish wings and two teeth, and kind of looks like a big fly.

Strategy

During the first phase, you can stay under it and attack upwards to send it up. It is also best to try to keep it in the middle of the battlefield so if you die, you can get your soul back before restarting the battle, and too far right will put objects in the way of combat. The Moorwing attacks will start slightly in a horizontal direction, allowing you to dash out of the way. If you have the drifter's cloak, you can float above the saws instead of jumping, making it much easier to avoid the double saws in phase 2. Remaining below the Moorwing while the circular saws are being thrown will also allow you to only jump once to avoid them. The best time to heal is generally while the Moorwing is making a multi-slash, or immediately after you avoid the side dive. Alternatively, you can pogo on top of the boss, greatly negating the difficulty of dodging all of its attacks

Trivia

You can fight Moorwing with Garmond and Zaza by requesting them to aid you in battle. You will have to request their aid every time you leave the game. Hornet is shown fighting Moorwing alongside Garmond and Zaza in the Xbox trailer. In the Hunter's Journal, Moorwing is described as being a mite-like creature. Moorwing's fight can be skipped if the Flea Caravan is moved there before Moorwing is killed. If Moorwing is fought in Act 3, a harder, Void-infused version of it can be fought.
