# Second Sentinel

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Second_Sentinel

The Second Sentinel is a boss in Hollow Knight: Silksong.

It's said in the actual moment that is an necessary boss and mission to enter the act 3 of the game, but in my gameplay i did this quest after entering act 3, so i think it's not necessary to go to act 3 and unlock the true ending. Unlock some dialog with npc in songclave that were helped by him after his activation. Necessary to gather all equipments.

To activate it you need the Cogwork Heart an item that you make after collecting 3 fragments, this fragments are collected in The Citadel acessory and prinicipal maps. To make the item you will need to gather all three fragments. To collect every fragmet you will need to resolve a puzzle that is hitting in sequence some bells.

1 is on the Memorium; 1 is on a chamber in The Citadel and the another one is on the Whispery Chamber.

When completed you can found a secret chamber in the area under the area of the elevator to the fight with Tessela and the Cogwork Dancers, ther you will  find a robot without a heart, when interacting with it you will have the option to insert the heart in it, making him turn alive. After that you can found him 2 times (one in the harmony saloon and the second near the supreme saloon) after seeing two times (uncertain that is necessary to see him the two times or just one is enough to activate it) you can find the quest final audience in the quest board of songclave .

After taking the quest you can find him in the supre saloon's and talk with him, activiate the boss fight, when winnning you earn an equipmente that can be used by hornet, named reserve bond.

(NOTE: I finished Act 3 and didn't know about this boss until I found it today, you don't have to fight it to trigger Act 3)
