# Sherma

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Sherma

Sherma is an NPC first revealed in the Hollow Knight: Silksong announcement trailer. He is a pilgrim on a spiritual journey who requires help from Hornet in certain situations.

Sherma makes two appearances in the announcement trailer. He is first encountered in the "New Friends" tab, standing within what appears to be a hot cavern hallway alongside Hornet. He clangs the two objects he carries together whilst singing, seemingly causing a large gate in front of the two to open.

He later appears briefly during the "New Towns" tab, resting next to a large structure amidst what appears to be the entrance to a large city, with many large structures visible in the background.

A later blog post directly mentioned him, describing him as "a pilgrim on a spiritual journey", with a "lack of combat and acrobatic prowess" which can land him in trouble.
