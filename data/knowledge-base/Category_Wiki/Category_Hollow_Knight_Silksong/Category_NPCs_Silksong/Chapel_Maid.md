# Chapel Maid

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Chapel_Maid

Chapel Maid is an NPC in Hollow Knight: Silksong . They appear to be carrying a large woodwind instrument.

Hornet meets the Chapel Maid just as she makes it out of the Moss Grotto from the Old Church and collapses. They act as a mentor of sorts throughout her journey in Pharloom . In the trailer, they are seen singing while Hornet plays her Needle .

In the Moss Grotto demo, they witness Hornet falling unconscious on the ground and start laughing, right before the end of the demo.
