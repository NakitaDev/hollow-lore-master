# Ballow

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Ballow

Ballow is an NPC in Hollow Knight: Silksong who is located in Deep Docks .

He is the assistant of Forge-Daughter and can be found shovelling coal into a metal carapace. He can be hit by Hornet , which deals no damage to him and makes Forge-Daughter laugh.

Dialogue

Location

Ballow is located in the same room as Forge-Daughter in Deep Docks.
