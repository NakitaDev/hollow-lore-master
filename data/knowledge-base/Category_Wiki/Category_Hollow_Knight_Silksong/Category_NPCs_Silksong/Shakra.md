# Shakra

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Shakra

Shakra is an NPC first revealed in the Hollow Knight: Silksong announcement trailer. She is a warrior who mastered the Craft-Skill of cartography. Shakra is seeking her master who disappeared.

Shakra makes a single appearance in the announcement trailer; she is shown under the "New Friends" tab. She is standing in a dark grey and foggy cavernous area dotted with large chains, standing next to what appears to be a large structure with visible windows. As Hornet approaches, Shakra lowers into a stance whilst making a loud vocalization.

She was later described in a blog post, noting her as part of a clan of warriors who each hone a "Craft-Skill", with hers being Cartography. It also explains that she is seeking her master, who disappeared under "strange circumstances", while also hinting of her role of aiding Hornet in her journey with her mastery of cartography.

In a later blog post, she was directly confirmed to be the source of mapping tools for Hornet, who will provide charts, compasses, and various other tools.

How she is located in the world is explained to be similar to how Cornifer could be found in Hollow Knight ; scattered golden rings signal her presence instead of scattered pages. If Shakra can't be found, she can be called by striking the ring on the platform to the right and above the bell station in Bellhart. Shakra refers to this area as her camp, where she returns to after she's done exploring and looking for her master.

Shakra is shown as a fighter in universe, she fights alongside hornet after the first wave in the ant arena in Hunter's march, if she's still in the area. She bounces her ring around to attack from a range. After the Trail's End wish is completed and Moorwing has been killed, Shakra can be dueled where Moorwing was fought.  She can also attack enemies that come near her, for example in Blasting Steps she can attack the flying enemies if they're lured near her.
