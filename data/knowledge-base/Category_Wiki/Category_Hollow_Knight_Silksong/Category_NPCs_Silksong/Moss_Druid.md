# Moss Druid

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Moss_Druid

The Moss Druid is an NPC in Hollow Knight: Silksong . They provide Hornet with a Gather Quest to collect Mossberries for the concoction within the cauldron that they are stirring. The bell tied upon the staff which they use is noted to be of some significance. The name of their Quest is "Mossberry Brew" and has the description of:

Sweetness! Sourness! Bitterness! I need it all with hastiness! Cruel world, will you not provide me with the fruits I desire?

Trivia

Entering the Druid's cauldron deals damage. The druid is located in the Moss Grotto.
