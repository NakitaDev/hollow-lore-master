# Rosaries

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Rosaries

Rosaries are the currency of Pharloom used in Hollow Knight: Silksong . Rosaries are visually represented by 3 variations that differ in value when dropped by enemies while in the player HUD and Inventory they are visually represented by the default red beads next to their count

Variations

There are 3 known variations of rosaries that differ in value.

Rosaries: The first variation of rosaries each is worth 1 bead Shell rosaries: The second variation of rosaries each is worth 5 beads Pearl rosaries: The final and highest valued variation of rosaries each is worth 15 beads

Rosary Strings

Rosaries can be strung together forming a Rosary String/Necklace, that store a number of rosaries and prevents them from loss upon death, Strung rosaries can't be spent until the Rosary string/necklace is broken

Use

Rosaries can be spent to buy items from the in-game NPC vendors, or can be spent on the Toll machines to unlock some Benches , Bell Beast stations or Doors thru-out Pharloom, additionally they can be used to acquire a map for the areas in the Citadel

Rosaries can be attached to strings, preventing them from being lost upon death. Strung Rosaries cannot be spent until the Rosary String is broken.

How to Acquire

Rosaries can be acquired in different ways:

Defeating enemies Rosary Caches NPC rewards Quest rewards at wish wells

There are currently four known ways of obtaining Rosary Strings:

Paying a vendor to put Rosary Beads onto a string Paying a Toll machine to put Rosary Beads on a string Collecting the ones scattered across the map Completing specific quests
