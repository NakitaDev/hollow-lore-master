# Far Fields

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Far_Fields

Far Fields is an Area in Act 1 of Hollow Knight: Silksong . It is accessible from the Deep Docks after beating Lace . It is a very green area, with plant-themed enemies. The bottom of the Far Fields also contains lava and explosive rocks.

Sub-Area: Weavenest Cindril

At the bottom east of the zone lies Weavenest Cindril. As with other Weavenests, it is blocked by a Weave Door requiring the Needolin skill to enter.
