# Hunter's March

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Hunter's_March

The Hunter's March is an Area in Hollow Knight: Silksong that is characterized by its anthill-like appearance. All its inhabitants are ants and ant warriors.

to access the H unter's March you must defeat the Skarrgard in the place right outside the Hunter's March. After beating the him you will come across a red flower you need to pogo off to get to the Hunter's March
