# Blasted Steps

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Blasted_Steps

The Blasted Steps are the last major area of Act 1 in Hollow Knight: Silksong . It is located close to the grand gate of Pharloom 's citadel. The area appears to be barren and dry, with strong winds, occasionally strong enough to push the player to the right while wearing the Drifter's Cloak
