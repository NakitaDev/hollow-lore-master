# Citadel

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Citadel

The Citadel is a late-game area in Pharloom . It is located at the very top of the kingdom. Hornet must ascend to the Citadel on her quest through Pharloom. The Citadel has been often described as "shining" and "holy".

In order to access the Citadel, Hornet must unlock 5 bells and ring them across the map. Upon doing so, the entrance to the Citadel will be accessible, although before entering, Hornet is interrupted by the Last Judge. After defeating the Last Judge, the Great Citadel will open.  If the entrance is reached before all the bells have been rung, an NPC will mark the locations on the map. additionally the citadel can be reached through The Mist.
