# Sinner's Road

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Sinner's_Road

Sinner's Road is an area that is largely defined by the Muckroaches that are used as lifestock and the bodies of water absolutely infested with maggots. The area is full of cages, used to contain the Roaches and the occasional prisoner, as well as technology that hasn't been maintained properly for a considerable amount of time. After defeating a group of Muckroaches, the NPC Styx can be found and he will give you a bench and grow Silkeaters every hour.
