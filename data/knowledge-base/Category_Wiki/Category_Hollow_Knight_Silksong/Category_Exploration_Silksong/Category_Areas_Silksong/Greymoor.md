# Greymoor

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Greymoor

On the way up to the Citadel at Pharloom's crest are the wasted caverns of Greymoor . The misted fields and dilapidated towers are now infested with Crawbugs, devious nesting types, and the former tenders of the land have become haunted.

Greymoor is described as a "huge" area, and one of the largest Team Cherry has ever created.
