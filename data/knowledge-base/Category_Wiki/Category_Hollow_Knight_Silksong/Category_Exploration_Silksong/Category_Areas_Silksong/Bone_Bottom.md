# Bone Bottom

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Bone_Bottom

Bone Bottom is a small pilgrim camp above the Moss Grotto in the Mosslands of Pharloom . It is the first hub settlement of Hollow Knight: Silksong , similar to Dirtmouth in Hollow Knight .

Pilgrims from abroad gather at Bone Bottom in the hopes of gaining entry through the nearby gate to The Marrow , the foundation of Pharloom and the first step in the traditional pilgrimage route to the Citadel .

Little else is known about this area beyond a mention within the reveal trailer that it has a resident named " Grunby of Bonebottom ".
