# The Mists

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/The_Mists

The Mist is a secret area accessed through Bilewater and a breakable wall in Sinners road . A treacherous illusion conjured by Phantom , the Mist serves to hide the Exhaust Organ used to expel the choking refuse of the Citadel .

Description

The entrance of the Mist is a pathway with several dead moorbugs like thread rakers, roach feeders and roach catchers scattered about

Apart from small groups of Silkflies the only lifeforms, if one could call them that, populating the area are Wraiths that hover motionlessly in the air until Hornet comes too close, at which point they will scream and lunge at her, draining her of life and silk upon making contact.

Blind movement will get her nowhere, but not all rooms are dangerous. Some smaller ones contain only Silkflies. If the Needolin is played near the Silkflies, they will guide Hornet towards the correct exit until she finally reaches the Exhaust Organ. If Hornet goes through the wrong exit or Falls into the mist-covered pits it will put Hornet back at the entrance to the mist.

Defeating Phantom will give you the Cross Stitch spell and the Mist will become inaccessible putting you straight infront of the exhaust organ. The exhaust organ will remain as a sub-area of Bilewater.

How to Access

The Mist is located at the bottom of  Bilewater, on the opposite side of the area's Bellway station. They additionally can be reached by breaking a wall on the left side of the left room after ascending the main room connected to the first bench in sinner’s road, connecting to the aforementioned entrance.
