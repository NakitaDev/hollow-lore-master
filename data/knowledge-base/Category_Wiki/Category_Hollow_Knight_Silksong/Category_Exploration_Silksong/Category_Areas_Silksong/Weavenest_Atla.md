# Weavenest Atla

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Weavenest_Atla

Weavenest Atla is an area south of Pharloom.

NPCs

Eva

Eva is located at the end of a secret passage, after a series of breakable walls. She can upgrade Hornet’s crests.

Enemies

Weavenest Atla is home to two Moss Mothers. After beating them, Hornet will receive a Weavelight.
