# Quests

Source: https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Quests

Quests (or tasks) are a system in Hollow Knight: Silksong that Hornet may accept from NPCs found across Pharloom . Ongoing Quests appear on notice boards that may be found in various towns.

These Quests are divided into categories:

Donate : Pay a toll, usually shell shards or Rosaries. Gather : Find and collect materials. Wayfarer : Related to locating or Helping a lost NPC. Learn : Gather information about the  world. Hunt : Killing a specific enemy or group of enemies or some other form of combat trial. Grand Hunt : killing a boss or a mini-boss . Sprint : Win a race against Sprintmaster Swift Witness : Find Mister Mushroom in various locations across Pharloom
